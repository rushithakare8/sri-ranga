About Sri Ranga Academy

Sri Ranga academy of fine arts found by Mr. Rajadurai in the year 2009 with the aim of promoting indian art in Rural. This academy was started in Thuraiyur (A small town) in Trichy district, Tamilnadu, india. It was started with 5 students in Bharathanatyam and then grown upto 300 students in various deciples like Vocal, violin, veenai, Mirudangam & drawing.

Bharatanatyam
